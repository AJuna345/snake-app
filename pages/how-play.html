Wrangler Training Manual

Master the art of the harvest. Avoid the walls. Don't bite yourself.

The Controls
Desktop

Use the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to navigate the snake across the grid.

Mobile / Touch

Tap the board in the direction you want the snake to turn, or use the on-screen D-Pad.

Power-Ups!

Power-Up Diamonds may appear after eating food. Move quick or it will disappear after 10 seconds!

◈
Super Snake

Doubles your score each time you eat food.

◈
Sneaky Snake

Slows down time to sneak around walls.

◈
Danger Walls

Power-ups can be risky! New walls might be added.

Wrangler Tip: Active effects last for 10 seconds. Check your Dashboard to see which power-up is currently engaged.
Customization

Access the Settings menu from the Dashboard to change your name, adjust game speed, or swap themes—including UNcaffeinated and UNA Pride 🦁.
